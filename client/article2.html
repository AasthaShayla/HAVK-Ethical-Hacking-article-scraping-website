How Cyber Security is different from Ethical Hacking?


Cyber Security is a vast subject that includes a lot of network and information security mechanisms, such as data security, digital forensics, Ethical Hacking and much more. Therefore, we can say that Ethical Hacking is a subpart of Cyber Security. In the case of Ethical Hacking, the ethical hacker hacks to protect the system. On the other hand, Cyber Security experts donâ€™t have to hack into the system. Their job is to protect he system by taking all possible protective measures. In the simplest of terms, Ethical Hackers make use of offensive security measures and cyber Security experts use defensive security measures.


Roles and resposibities of ethical hacker


  • An ethical hacker must seek authorization from the organization that owns the system. Hackers should obtain complete approval before performing any security assessment on the system or network.


  • They determine the scope of their assessment and make known their plan to the organization..


  • They keep their discoveries confidential. As their purpose is to secure the system or network, ethical hackers should agree to and respect their non-disclosure agreement


  • They erase all traces of the hack after checking the system for any vulnerability. It prevents malicious hackers from entering the system through the identified loopholes.
